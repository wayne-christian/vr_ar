The Module

Its new

Its cool

Its optional (For all)

It will teach students to develop and design applications for Virtual and Augmented Reality (VR/AR) platforms

Consider different areas of applications, including training, simulation, medical/therapeutic, games, and other interactive experiences

Theoretical aspects of design for VR/AR systems, including topics like

Locomotion and control methods

Representation of virtual worlds and non-human characters

Look at limitations such as simulator sickness, and accessibility, and how to evaluate user experience

This module assumes no prior knowledge of VR/AR development tools, nor games design

Focus is on generic VR design themes, and most examples of applications are not games related

Structure

1 x hour Lecture

2 x hour Workshop

Tools

Programming in C#

No prior knowledge needed
